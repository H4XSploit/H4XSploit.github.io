eSign Installation Guide

UPDATED

Follow these three simple steps to complete the eSign installation:

Step 1: Install the DNS configuration. 🔗 Download Khovind’s DNS ⬇️
Step 2: Download and install eSign. ⚡ Download eSign 🔥
Step 3: Download the certificate and upload it to eSign. 🔒 Download Certificates 🔥😊

⚠️ Note: If you are unable to install a certificate in eSign, your device may have been blacklisted. You can either:

  • 🔄 Reset your iPhone to factory settings
  • ⭐ Get a premium certificate
